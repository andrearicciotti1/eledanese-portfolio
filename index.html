This is me

After almost seven years as a Graphic Designer and Art Director — focused on music, fashion and advertising — and nearly fifteen years working in the music industry in different roles, I've focused solely on music supervision for the last few years.

My skill set covers creative research, budget management, negotiation of syncs directly with rights holders, licensing, soundtrack finalization, and cue sheet drafting — staying in open communication with everyone involved in producing a film or series, from writers and directors to editors and marketing.

I've worked with all the major streaming and TV platforms — Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, Sky — and with several of Italy's leading production companies. Alongside this, I write songs as Kuni; my debut EP came out via Factory Flaws in June 2023, two more singles followed in 2024, and more are coming.
